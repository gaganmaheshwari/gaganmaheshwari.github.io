    Gagan Maheshwari's Biography

      1 minute read

    TL;DR
    Technophile
    Polyglot
    Change Agent
    Entrepreneur


    Gagan Maheshwari is a seasoned techie who has extensive experience building highly scalable, available and awesome products in several fast-paced startups (both internal and external). He can frequently be found exploring and playing with new technologies, frameworks and ideas.

    Gagan is an expert in the areas of Payments, Tokenization, Commerce, Developer Experience, API Design & Strategy, Distributed Computing, and Cloud Computing.

    Gagan currently works at Paypal where he is changing the game through delivering superior developer experience and transforming the PayPal sandbox experience for App developers.

    Prior to PayPal, he worked for Equinix where he helped architect, build and deliver the Equinix Cloud Exchange platform from sratch up. The cloud exchange platform has made waves in the cloud and inter-connection industry and has helped un-lock the full potential of cloud computing.

    Over the years, Gagan has built and led several transformational and high value product deliveries at IBM Software Lab and ADP among others. He has also acquired a respectable consulting experience, working for the “BIG 5” - BearingPoint Business Consulting (erstwhile KPMG Consulting).

    Throughout this journey, Gagan has enjoyed bringing new and disruptive ideas to the fore and got stuff done - Helpful, beautiful and meaningful stuff.
